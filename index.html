Welcome to BioBase Alpha!

BioBase Alpha is a cutting-edge initiative dedicated to advancing sustainable agriculture in extraterrestrial environments. Our mission is to develop innovative solutions that ensure reliable food production for long-duration space missions, supporting human life beyond Earth while promoting environmental sustainability.

About

BioBase Alpha is a robot that plants barley in space in special greenhouses with appropriate conditions. It provides food that is healthy and rich in nutriments for scientists, astronauts and cosmonauts in the ISS. With a VEX IQ drivebase, and a robotic arm with different mechanisms, our robot can treat all steps of cultivation. And with temperature, humidity and light sensors, it can detect any change in conditions and/or a problem in the system. BioBase Alpha also treats, analyses information and takes autonomous decisions since it's AI enabled. Briefly, our solution is a complex agricultural system in space and has a lot of benefits.
