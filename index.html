Welcome to BioBase Alpha

BioBase Alpha is a forward-thinking initiative focused on revolutionizing space agriculture to support life in extraterrestrial environments. Join us in shaping the future of sustainability.

About

BioBase Alpha is a robot that seems food in spacein special greenhouses with appropriate conditions. It provides food that is healthy and rich in nutriments for scientists, astronauts and cosmonauts in the ISS. With a VEX IQ drivebase, and a robot arm with different mechanismes, our robot can treat all steps of culture. And with temperature, humidity and light sensors, it can detect any change in conditions and/or problem in the system. BioBase Alpha also treats, analyses information and takes autonomus desicions since it's AI enabled. Brievly, our solution is a complex agriculture system in space, and has a lot of benefits.
