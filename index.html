Welcome to BioBase Alpha

BioBase Alpha is a forward-thinking initiative focused on revolutionizing space agriculture to support life in extraterrestrial environments. Join us in shaping the future of sustainability.

About

BioBase Alpha is designed to grow healthy food in space using smart agricultural technologies. It supports astronauts on long missions by offering fresh, nutritious produce and reducing dependence on preserved food.

Our Team

We are a passionate group of young innovators, scientists, and engineers driven by curiosity and guided by sustainability goals. Our work spans research, design, and real-world prototyping of space farming systems.

Specifications & Features

  • Hydroponic and soil-free growth systems
  • AI-controlled environment monitoring
  • Modular and space-efficient design
  • Real-time health tracking of plants

Impact

Our project contributes to the UN SDG 3: Good Health and Well-being by ensuring astronauts access fresh food, boosting their mental and physical health. It's also a model for sustainable agriculture on Earth.
